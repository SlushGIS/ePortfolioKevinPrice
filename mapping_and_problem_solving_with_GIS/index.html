Mapping and Problem Solving with GIS

The following report was created as part of a course lesson in which we performed a hypothetical site selection scenario with tailored data for the purpose of helping a client establishing a vineyard. The analysis steps for this lesson were predetermined and served as walk through process for conducting such an analysis. However, I additionally included existing vineyard sites in the area from a publicly availble source. This was not a part of the assignment but demonstrates my knowledge of and ability to find and utilizes publicly available data. It also serves as an important acknowledgment that no analysis is perfect and additional information or different ways of interpreting information can drastically change the results of an analysis.
